# Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
Interactive dashboard to explore Superstore sales data, including insights on sales, profit, discounts, shipping, and customer segments.

## Key Business Questions
1. Which markets and regions generate the most sales and profit?
2. Which product categories or sub-categories perform best?
3. How do discounts impact profitability?
4. What shipping modes and priorities are most commonly used?
5. Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
